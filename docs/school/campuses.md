## Paris - Kremlin-Bicêtre (PRS-KB)

This campus is located at **14-16 rue Voltaire, 94270 Le Kremlin-Bicêtre**.

This is the Paris campus that hosts students in the engineering cycle (ING1, ING2, and ING3).

### Available Majors
- SRS (*Security, Network et Security*)
- Cybersecurity and systems (full work-study)
- Web development and new applications (full work-study)
- CP (*Platform Design*)
- MTI (*Multimedia & New Communications*)
- SCIA (*AI - Data Science*)
- Quantum (*Quantum computing and technologies*)
- TCOM (*Telecom networks*)
- Cloud computing (2nd and 3rd year work-study)
- GITM (*Global IT Management*, major in English)
- SIGL (*Information Systems and Software Engineering*)
- Image
